Privacy Policy

Introduction and organizational info

We, at Bernardo Barosa (TasteRoots), are dedicated to serving our customers and contacts to the best of our abilities. Part of our commitment involves the responsible management of personal information collected through the TasteRoots mobile application, this website, and any related interactions.

Our primary goals in processing this information include:

  • Enhancing the user experience on our platform by understanding customer needs and preferences.
  • Providing timely support and responding to inquiries or service requests.
  • Improving our products and services to meet the evolving demands of our users.
  • Conducting necessary business operations, such as billing and account management.

It is our policy to process personal information with the utmost respect for privacy and security. We adhere to all relevant regulations and guidelines to ensure that the data we handle is protected against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

We do not have a designated Data Protection Officer (DPO) but remain fully committed to addressing your privacy concerns. Should you have any questions or require further information about how we manage personal information, please contact us:

Email: tasteroots@barosa.app

Scope and application

This privacy policy is designed to protect the personal information of all our stakeholders, including website visitors, registered users, and customers. This policy outlines our practices and your rights related to personal information.

Data storage and protection

Data storage

Personal information is stored in secure servers. For services that require international data transfer, we ensure compliance with applicable laws and equivalent data protection standards.

Data hosting partners: We partner with reputable data hosting providers that adhere to stringent data protection standards.

Data protection measures

  • Encryption: Data is protected during transfer and at rest using robust encryption technologies.
  • Access control: Access is restricted to authorized personnel with legitimate business purposes.

Data processing agreements

When sharing data with third-party providers, we do so under Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) ensuring compliance with GDPR and other applicable laws.

Transparency and control

We believe in transparency and providing you control over your personal information. You will be informed of significant changes and, where applicable, given consent options.

User rights and choices

In accordance with GDPR and other applicable laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR)
  • Right to lodge a complaint (Art. 77 GDPR)

To exercise these rights, contact us via email or phone. We may need to verify your identity to protect your data.

Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to ensure website functionality and improve user experience.

Types of cookies

  • Essential cookies
  • Performance and analytics cookies
  • Functional cookies
  • Advertising and targeting cookies

Upon first visit, you may accept all cookies, reject non-essential ones, or customize preferences.

Compliance with United States privacy laws

US residents may appeal decisions regarding privacy requests within 60 days via email or phone.

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

  • Right to Know

  • Right to Limit sensitive data usage

Sensitive and biometric data

Sensitive personal data is processed only with prior consent and only for disclosed purposes. Consent may be withdrawn at any time.
